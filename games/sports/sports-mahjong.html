Sports Mahjong

About Sports Mahjong

Sports Mahjong is a puzzle game where you need to fill in the grid with the correct colors to solve the puzzle.

How to Play

Use arrow keys or WASD to move your spaceship. Press SPACE to shoot. Collect power-ups to upgrade your weapons and defeat the alien invaders.
